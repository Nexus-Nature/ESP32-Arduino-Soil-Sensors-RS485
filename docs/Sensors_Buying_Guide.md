## Why did Sensor #6 win?
During our **Gravimetric Calibration Test**, Sensor #6 was the only unit that provided a near-linear response to actual water volume changes. While other sensors (like Sensor #1 and #5) exaggerated the moisture levels (showing >90% when the real moisture was 46%), Sensor #6 successfully read `54.3%`, which is statistically the closest to our `46.0%` reference benchmark.
